An open-source Quasi Direct Drive Actuator for use in locomotion centric projects.


**Note: The project is ongoing, the blog post will be updated as the project progresses.** 

# Hardware
- 400kv BLDC
- GEABN0.5-20-8-K-6
- SI0.5-100
- HK0408
- SSG0.5-40B
- SB6700ZZ
